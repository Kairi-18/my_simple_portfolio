Laing

Ingredients

  • 3.5 oz taro leaves (dried)
  • 6 cups coconut milk
  • 2 cups coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup shrimp paste (bagoong or balaw)
  • 1/2 lb. pork shoulder, thinly sliced
  • 5 to 7 pieces red chilies
  • 1 piece onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced ginger
  • 8 cloves garlic, crushed

    • Directions

    1. Combine coconut milk, pork, shrimp paste, ginger, onion, and garlic in a cooking pot. Heat and let boil.
    2. Once boiling, gently stir. Cover and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    3. Add the dried taro leaves. Do not stir. Let them absorb the coconut milk for about 20 to 30 minutes.
    4. Once absorbed, stir the leaves and cook for 10 more minutes.
    5. Pour in the coconut cream. Add the red chilies and stir. Cook for 10 to 12 minutes.
    6. Transfer to a serving plate and enjoy!
