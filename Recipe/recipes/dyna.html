Dynamite Lumpia

Ingredients

  • 8 pieces long green pepper
  • 4 ounces cheddar cheese
  • 8 pieces lumpia wrapper
  • 2 cups cooking oil
  • 6 ounces ground pork
  • 1 piece onion, minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed and minced
  • 1 piece egg
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Heat 3 tablespoons oil in a pan. Sauté garlic and onion until soft.
  2. Add ground pork and sauté until medium brown. Season with salt and ground black pepper.
  3. Remove from the pan and transfer to a bowl. Let it cool.
  4. Beat the egg and mix it into the cooked ground pork. Set aside.
  5. Slice one side of the peppers lengthwise and remove the seeds.
  6. Slice the cheddar cheese into long strips and stuff into each pepper.
  7. Fill the peppers with the cooked meat mixture.
  8. Wrap the stuffed peppers in lumpia wrappers.
  9. Heat 2 cups of oil in a pan. Fry each lumpia in medium heat for about 2 minutes per side or until golden brown.
  10. Remove from the pan and place on a wire rack. Let cool before serving.
  11. Enjoy with your favorite dipping sauce!
