Mango Graham

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of crushed graham
  • 20 pieces Graham crackers
  • 1 cup all-purpose cream or Cool Whip
  • 3/4 cup condensed milk
  • 2 cups of mangoes, thinly sliced

    • Directions

    1. In a rectangular dish, arrange 8 to 10 pieces of graham crackers. Set aside.
    2. In a bowl, combine the cream/Cool Whip and condensed milk. Mix well.
    3. On the layered crackers at the bottom, spread the milk and cream mixture.
    4. Spread out the thinly sliced mangoes evenly on top of the cream.
    5. Make another layer of graham crackers; spread the cream and mango slices.
      You can do multiple layers if desired.
    6. Garnish the top layer with mango slices and sprinkle with crushed graham.
    7. Chill for at least 3 hours before serving.
